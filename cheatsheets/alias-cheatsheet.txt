LINUX
======

## Show all active aliases
  alias

## Make temp alias (non-persistent)
  alias new='old'

## Make permanent alias
  Depends on your shell
  If bash, check ~/.bashrc

## Remove alias
  unalias aliasname

## Kill em all
  unalias -a



WINDOWS
=======

## Set
Set-Alias -Name which -Value Get-Command

## List
Alias

## Find
Get-Alias -Name theAliasName
